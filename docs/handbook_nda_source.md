CLOG BUSTERZ, LLC
Richmond, Kentucky | Serving Central Kentucky
EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK SUMMARY
COPY FOR REVIEW ONLY -- NOT A SIGNING DOCUMENT
Prepared for: | 06/27/2026 at 5:20:43 PM EDT
IMPORTANT: This document summarizes the policies contained in the Clog Busterz Employee Handbook. By initialing each section and signing the
acknowledgment, you confirm that you have reviewed the full handbook and agree to comply with all policies.
1. EMPLOYMENT AT WILL
Employment is at-will. Company reserves right to modify policies. Information is confidential per NDA.
2. WELCOME
Welcome to Clog Busterz LLC. We emphasize trust, reliability, and high-quality workmanship.
3. EMPLOYEE ORIENTATION
New employees receive performance requirements and Company policy information. Falsification of documents may result in termination.
4. EQUAL EMPLOYMENT OPPORTUNITY
Company complies with all federal, state, and local EEO laws. No discrimination on any protected basis.
5. ANTI-DISCRIMINATION & HARASSMENT
Strictly prohibited. Report immediately. No retaliation. Violators subject to termination.
6. SAFETY RULES & REGULATIONS
Safety is a condition of employment. Report all incidents immediately. OSHA compliance required.
7. 811 BEFORE YOU DIG
All employees MUST comply with the Kentucky Underground Facility Damage Prevention Act (KRS 367.4901-367.4917). Before ANY
excavation or digging you MUST call 811 at least 2 business days in advance. Failure to call 811 before digging is a violation of state law. If
utility lines have NOT been marked, STOP WORK and contact your manager immediately.
8. DRUG, ALCOHOL, AND CONTRABAND POLICY
Zero tolerance for illegal substances on Company premises or vehicles. Random testing. Firearms prohibited except lawfully stored in locked
personal vehicles.
9. EMAIL AND INTERNET USAGE
No personal texting during work. All network traffic is Company property. No expectation of privacy. NDA covers all electronic
communications.
10. VEHICLE POLICY
BUSINESS ONLY. No personal use, no passengers, no pets, no alcohol. TEXTING WHILE DRIVING = ZERO TOLERANCE = IMMEDIATE
TERMINATION. Employee personally liable for driving with invalid license.
11. COMPANY TECHNOLOGY & iPAD POLICY
Devices are Company property. No personal apps, no screenshots, no modifications. All data is confidential per NDA.
12. GAS CARD & FUEL POLICY
Business use only. Gas over 10% of revenue triggers review. Misuse = theft.
13. SOLICITATION
No solicitation by non-employees. Employees only during non-work time in non-work areas.

14. COMPANY PROPERTY
No removal for personal use. Return all property upon termination including iPads, phones, tools, keys, gas cards.
15. CONFIDENTIALITY OF INTERNAL SYSTEMS
All proprietary systems are trade secrets. No disclosure, screenshots, or copying. All sheets contain invisible digital watermarks traceable to
the employee. NDA governs.
16. TERMINATION
At-will. Final paycheck per KRS. Return all Company property on last day.
17. ATTENDANCE AND PUNCTUALITY
Report punctually. Notify supervisor of absences. Excessive tardiness or absenteeism = disciplinary action.
18. ON-CALL & HOLIDAY POLICY
On-Call No-Show = forfeit holiday pay. Weekend Crew mandatory. 5 paid holidays per year.
19. PAYROLL DEDUCTION & CLAWBACK
Employee authorizes deductions for: callback penalties (50%/100%), equipment damage, unreturned property, gas card misuse, bonus
clawback, overpayments, training costs (within 90 days). Minimum wage floor ($7.25/hr KY) always protected.
20. RECEIPT & DOCUMENTATION REQUIREMENTS
Submit same-day: receipts, photos, video, call recording. Pay held until documentation submitted. Fraudulent documentation = immediate
termination.
21. CALLBACK POLICY
Tier 1 No Fault: no penalty. Tier 2 Half-Ass Work: 50% of original job pay deducted. Tier 3 Turd Job: 100% of original job pay deducted +
mandatory 8AM meeting with manager. Materials on ALL callbacks charge the ORIGINAL tech. 2+ callbacks/week = ALL awards disqualified
for the week. Callback counter tracked on your sheet — patterns trigger intervention.
22. INCENTIVE & AWARDS PROGRAM
Discretionary. Corn Crown: $6,500+55% margin (commission) / $7,500+55% (salary). Golden Turd: $9,500+55% margin (commission) /
$11,000+55% (salary). Crown Bonus: $150. Turd Bonus: $250. FloodBusterz: $225. Relines: $200/$400. BioOne: $20. All thresholds
confidential per NDA.
23. MANDATORY MEDIATION
Disputes submitted to mandatory mediation in Madison County, Kentucky per AAA rules. Costs shared equally.
24. VENUE & JURISDICTION
Circuit Court of Madison County or U.S. District Court Eastern District of Kentucky.
25. WAIVER OF JURY TRIAL
EMPLOYEE AND COMPANY EACH WAIVE ALL RIGHTS TO JURY TRIAL for any action related to employment.
26. ATTORNEYS FEES
Employee pays Company court costs and attorney fees if Company must litigate to enforce.
27. GOVERNING LAW
Commonwealth of Kentucky law without conflict of laws principles.
28. MANAGEMENT VERIFICATION
All infractions confirmed by manager. If manager lied to protect employee, BOTH are immediately terminated.
| Clog Busterz, LLC | 06/27/2026 | CONFIDENTIAL
